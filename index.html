Welcome!

We are a research group exploring the propagation of elastic waves in a wide range of unconventional materials, including soft structures, granular media, topological lattices, and nonlinear metamaterials. Our work focuses on model systems to observe striking nonlinear wave phenomena, such as bifurcations, soliton propagation, rogue waves, and domino-like transition waves. Conversely, we design functional structures aimed at controlling elastic wave propagation for applications such as wave filtering, mitigation, guiding, or energy harvesting.
